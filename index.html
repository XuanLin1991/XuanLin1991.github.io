Research

My work lies at the intersection of machine learning, graph neural networks, and bioinformatics. We are interested in:

  • Text-based molecule generation
  • LLM for chemical synthesis
  • Drug-target interaction / binding affinity prediction
  • Drug-drug interaction event prediction
