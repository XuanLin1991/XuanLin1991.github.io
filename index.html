About Me

I am an associate professor in the School of Computer Science, Xiangtan University (XTU), P. R. China. Prior to joining XTU, I received my Ph.D. degree from Hunan University in 2021. I was a visiting scholar at University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), working with Prof. Philip S. Yu.

My research interests include multi-modal learning, large language models, and AI for drug discovery. I have published more than 20 technical papers in top conferences and journals.

Research Interests

  • ▹ Text-based molecule generation
  • ▹ LLM for chemical synthesis
  • ▹ Drug-target interaction / binding affinity prediction
  • ▹ Drug-drug interaction event prediction
